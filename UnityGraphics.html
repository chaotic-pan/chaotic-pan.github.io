Unity Graphics Project

Overview

This project was an Uni assignment, I created in cooperation with a fellow student.
The goal was to get familiar with Unitys graphics system, by creating a few different FVX Shader Graphs, Visual Effects Graphs, and parametric surfaces.

Work Distribution

Besides the displaying room and the first-person player controls, I also created the Lightning Bolt, Fairy Dust, Fire Smoke, Hologram Shader, and the Hourglass, Snail and Dented Cube Surfaces.
